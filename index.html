Visual Learning Lab

Children's Drawing Explorers

Interactive tools for exploring a large dataset of children's drawings — tens of thousands of sketches across dozens of object categories, produced by children ages 2–10. Browse how drawings of the same concept change with age, watch them unfold stroke by stroke, and see how a model recognizes them.

Data collected through interactive drawing games at the San Jose Children's Discovery Museum and online.

Watch drawings draw themselves one stroke at a time, with each stroke colored by its semantic part (body, wing, wheel…) and inter-annotator agreement. Compare two categories side by side.
